Fried Rice

Ingredients:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice, preferably chilled
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (such as peas, carrots, and corn)
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add beaten eggs and cook until scrambled. Remove eggs from skillet and set aside.
  2. Add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil to the skillet. Add mixed vegetables and cook until tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add chopped green onions and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps. Stir-fry for 3-5 minutes until the rice is heated through.
  5. Return the scrambled eggs to the skillet. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper. Stir-fry everything together until well combined and heated through.
  6. Adjust seasoning to taste, adding more soy sauce or salt if needed.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot. Enjoy your homemade Fried Rice!
