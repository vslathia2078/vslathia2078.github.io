Dish 1:Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 4 fish fillets (such as cod or haddock)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of American cheese
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon sweet pickle relish
  • 1 tablespoon yellow mustard
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°F (93°C) to keep the cooked fillets warm while you prepare the rest of the sandwiches.
  2. Combine mayonnaise, sweet pickle relish, mustard, vinegar, sugar, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper in a small bowl to make the tartar sauce. Mix well and refrigerate until ready to use.
  3. Set up your breading station: Place flour in one shallow dish, beaten eggs in another, and breadcrumbs in a third dish.
  4. Pat fish fillets dry with paper towels and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Coat each fish fillet in flour, then dip in beaten eggs, and finally coat with breadcrumbs, pressing gently to adhere.
  6. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the breaded fish fillets until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels and keep warm in the preheated oven.
  7. Toast the hamburger buns in a toaster or on a skillet until lightly golden.
  8. Assemble the sandwiches: Spread tartar sauce on the bottom half of each bun, place a slice of cheese on top, followed by a fried fish fillet, and finally, the top half of the bun.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade Filet-o-Fish sandwiches!
