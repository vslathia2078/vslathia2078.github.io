Mash Patatos

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes (such as Russet or Yukon Gold)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into chunks.
  2. Place the potato chunks in a large pot and cover them with cold water. Add a pinch of salt to the water.
  3. Bring the water to a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to medium and let the potatoes simmer until they are fork-tender, about 15-20 minutes.
  4. While the potatoes are cooking, heat the milk and butter in a small saucepan over low heat until the butter is melted and the mixture is warm.
  5. Once the potatoes are cooked, drain them and return them to the pot.
  6. Mash the potatoes using a potato masher or fork until they reach your desired consistency.
  7. Gradually pour the warm milk and butter mixture into the mashed potatoes, stirring until the potatoes are creamy and smooth. Add more milk if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  8. Season the mashed potatoes with salt and black pepper to taste, and mix until well combined.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade mashed potatoes!
